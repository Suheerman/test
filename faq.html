Your trusted financial partner in Toronto. Here are answers to some common questions.

2. I'm a small business in Toronto. Do I really need professional payroll management?

+

Absolutely. Payroll is one of the most critical and complex aspects of running a business, even a small one. It involves more than just paying employees; you need to manage deductions, remittances to CRA, T4s, Records of Employment, and stay compliant with constantly evolving labour laws. Our payroll management service eliminates the risk of costly errors and penalties, saves you significant time, and ensures your employees are paid accurately and on time, allowing you to focus on your core business in Toronto.

3. How can Gugan Accounting help me save money on my personal (T1) or corporate taxes?

+

Our approach goes beyond just filling out forms. For Personal (T1) taxes, we meticulously identify every eligible deduction and credit you might qualify for, often uncovering savings missed by DIY filers. For Corporate Tax, we work proactively throughout the year on tax planning strategies, optimizing your business expenses, asset depreciation, and compensation structures to legally minimize your tax liability and maximize your bottom line, specifically considering Canadian tax regulations.

4. I'm new to business in Toronto. Can you help me with business registration?

+

Yes, absolutely! Navigating the initial steps of business registration can be complex, involving provincial (Ontario) and federal requirements. We provide comprehensive guidance through the entire process, helping you choose the right legal structure (sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation), register your business name, obtain necessary federal and provincial tax accounts (like HST), and ensure all initial compliance steps are met so you can launch your Toronto business confidently and correctly.

5. What's the difference between bookkeeping and accounting, and which do I need?

+

Bookkeeping is the systematic recording of financial transactions (your day-to-day data entry). Accounting takes that recorded information and analyzes, interprets, and reports on it to provide a bigger financial picture for strategic decision-making and tax compliance. Most businesses need both! Gugan Accounting offers integrated services where our expert bookkeepers ensure accurate records, and our accountants use that data to provide valuable insights, tax planning, and overall financial strategy, giving you a complete financial solution.

6. What is HST, and why is filing it correctly so important for my business?

+

HST (Harmonized Sales Tax) is a consumption tax charged on most goods and services in Ontario. For businesses, correct HST filing is crucial for several reasons: it ensures you properly collect and remit the tax to the CRA, claim your Input Tax Credits (ITCs) accurately to reduce your own HST payable, and avoid significant penalties or audits. Our precise HST filing services mean you stay compliant, keep more of your hard-earned money, and maintain a good standing with the tax authorities.

7. What is WSIB and why do I need to report it?

+

WSIB (Workplace Safety and Insurance Board) provides no-fault workplace insurance for employers and workers in Ontario. If you have employees or operate in certain industries, you are legally required to register and regularly report your insurable earnings to WSIB. Accurate and timely WSIB reporting is essential to avoid penalties, ensure your workers are covered in case of injury, and protect your business from potential liabilities. We simplify this complex reporting process for you.
